About Me

Hello! I'm Mary-Anne Schaffers, a passionate front-end developer based in South Africa with a strong background in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. My love for coding began with the desire to create visually engaging and functional websites that provide users with seamless experiences.

Over the last few months, I've worked on a variety of projects that span multiple industries, from tech-driven applications to yoga platforms and advisory services. Every project I work on is an opportunity to learn and refine my skills, ensuring that I bring the best results to the table.

One of my core beliefs is that you are never too old or too young to start coding. Whether you're a complete beginner or someone looking to refine your skills, the world of web development offers endless possibilities. I started my journey later than most, but it's been incredibly rewarding, and I'm proof that it's never too late to learn something new.

When I'm not coding, you can find me exploring new technologies, working on personal development, or sharing my knowledge with others. Whether you're looking to build your first website or take your skills to the next level, I'm here to help! All made possible with the structured learning though SheCodes.
